## Full Description

StudySphere is a smart student productivity hub built for university life. It helps students organize assignments, plan study sessions, track exams, manage notes, revise with flashcards, and stay focused with a built-in timer.

Use StudySphere to turn academic pressure into a clear plan. Create tasks, build schedules, track progress, prepare for exams, and use Sage, the in-app guide, to find the right tool when you are not sure where to start.

Key features:

- Student dashboard with tasks, reminders, progress, and exam countdowns
- Smart planner and Study Autopilot
- Focus timer and streak tracking
- Notes, flashcards, grades, files, and groups
- Weekly reports and revision support
- Brain-break games for short study breaks
- In-app guide assistant named Sage

StudySphere is designed for students who want structure without losing momentum.
